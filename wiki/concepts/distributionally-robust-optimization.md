# Distributionally Robust Optimization

Distributionally Robust Optimization (DRO) involves optimizing expectations over the worst-case realization of a probability measure within a defined uncertainty set or constraint budget. The dual representation of [[CVaR]] directly links risk-averse optimization to DRO.
