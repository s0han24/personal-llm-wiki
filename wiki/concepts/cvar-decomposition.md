# CVaR Decomposition

CVaR Decomposition refers to breaking down the conditional CVaR over a time horizon into iterative steps, often by mapping confidence levels to augmented states. While foundational for integrating [[CVaR]] into Markov Decision Processes, some early Bellman Operators targeting this (such as Chow et al. 2015) were proven flawed.
