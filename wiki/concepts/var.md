# Value at Risk (VaR)
Value at Risk (VaR) at a confidence level $\alpha$ is a risk measure indicating a threshold such that the probability of the return falling below it is exactly $\alpha$. Unlike [[CVaR]], it is a quantile threshold, not an expectation of the tail distributions beyond that threshold, and lacks coherence as a risk measure.
