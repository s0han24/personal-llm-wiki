# Conditional Value at Risk (CVaR)

Conditional Value at Risk (CVaR) is a prominent risk measure used in finance and operations. It measures the expected payoff over the $\alpha$% worst outcomes. Unlike Value at Risk (VaR), CVaR is a coherent risk measure that quantifies the expected loss in the tail of the distribution.
