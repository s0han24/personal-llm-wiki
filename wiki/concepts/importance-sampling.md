# Importance Sampling

Importance Sampling (IS) is a variance reduction technique used in Monte-Carlo estimation. It involves sampling from a different distribution and suitably modifying the estimator to remain unbiased. In sequential decision-making models, a *model-based* importance sampling alters the MDP transition probabilities, requiring an environment simulator.
